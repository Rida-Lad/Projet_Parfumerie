Clients Recreations

Ocean Breeze

A refreshing fragrance inspired by the waves and salty air of the sea. Experience the crisp, clean scent that transports you to a coastal paradise with notes of sea salt, citrus, and aquatic minerals. Perfect for those seeking an escape to the ocean's serene beauty.

Forest Whisper

Earthy notes of pine and cedarwood bring the calm of the forest. Experience the tranquility of a misty woodland with hints of moss, vetiver, and fresh earth. A scent that grounds and rejuvenates the spirit.

Rose Elegance

Romantic floral tones with a timeless touch of rose petals. A sophisticated blend of Bulgarian rose, peony, and subtle musk that evokes luxury and grace. Perfect for evening wear and special occasions.

Citrus Spark

Bright and zesty notes of lemon and orange for an energizing lift. This vibrant fragrance combines bergamot, grapefruit, and mandarin with a hint of ginger for a refreshing, invigorating experience that awakens the senses.

Vanilla Dream

Warm, sweet vanilla blended with subtle spices for cozy evenings. A comforting scent with notes of Madagascar vanilla, tonka bean, and caramel, softened by whispers of sandalwood and amber. Like a warm embrace on a cold night.
